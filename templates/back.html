    BACK MUSCLES WORKOUT

    To effectively target the back muscles, incorporate a variety of exercises that engage different muscle groups within the back. Start with compound movements like deadlifts or pull-ups to activate multiple muscles simultaneously. Deadlifts target the lower back, while pull-ups engage the upper back and lats. Move on to rows, whether with a barbell, dumbbells, or a cable machine, to further target the lats, rhomboids, and traps. Ensure proper form and focus on pulling with the back muscles rather than using momentum. Include exercises like lat pulldowns and seated rows to isolate specific areas of the back and achieve a balanced development.
